Snickerdoodle

Description

Ingrediants

  1. 1 cup sugar
  2. 2 large eggs
  3. 1 teaspoon Cream of Tartar
  4. 1 teaspoon salt
  5. 2 teaspoons vanilla
  6. 1 teaspoon baking soda

Steps

  1. Start by creaming butter and sugar for 4-5 minutes.
  2. Use Cream of Tartar as a leavening agent.
  3. Double roll the cookie balls into a cinnamon-sugar mixture.
  4. Bake just until the edges begin to become a light golden color.
  5. To serve, carefully invert on serving plate with edges when completely cool.
